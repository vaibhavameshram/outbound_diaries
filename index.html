ABOUT US

The Office of International Relations (OIR) works to promote exchange of students faculty and ideas between IIT Kharagpur and her international partners. The Office has a dedicated team of Executives working on all aspects of international engagement and is headed by the Dean of International Relations. Adiministrative support is also provided by the Institutional Development Office. The International Relations Cell (IRC) is a student body working under the OIR. Together OIR and IRC work towards increasing IIT Kharagpur's global outreach.
